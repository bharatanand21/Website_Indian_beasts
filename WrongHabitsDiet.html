Things You do Wrong:

With a natural instinct,People usually do what they enjoy doing.This the case with eating habits also.But by following some of the healthy habits you can naturally boost your metabolism and achieve the desired goals.Some these are:

1.Drink Water:

Drink plenty of water and needless to say as you wake up is a must.Never drink water before or after 30 mins (20 also works). Ya ya you would have heard this a lot but believe me this really works try it out as an experiment for a week,you surely will observe changes.

2.Eating Habit:

Use smaller utensils.if you don’t have then buy for at least yourself only.This will prevent overeating which is bad for you either you are cutting or gaining.

3.Avoid Skipping Meals:

Never skip meals especially breakfast although crash diet also helps but proper knowledge is required.

4.Sleeping Cycle:

Check your sleeping patterns. Don’t remain woke up till late night. This only reduces your capabilities of doing various day to day tasks.Further reducing energy.

5.Masturbation:

This one is very important but needless to say how important was to include this I.e masturbate lesser. You are yourself aware that whether it’s ok or too much. It take too much of hormones and energy from the body .(Plus guilt xD)

6.Eat Protein:

Eat protein especially in the breakfast as it makes us full aq little longer and prevents cravings to eat.It also curbs the secretion of a hormone said to create hunger sensation

7.Eat in Proportions:

This one is most crucial one.Our usual habit make us eat to full to throat.Thats totally wrong instead take 4-5 meals a day in small proportions(Taking atleast 4 are damm easy)

8.Lesser Cheat Meals:

Now it is a thing some might disagree as they seek it as a reward for a week long trainiing and diet.But my friend this helps in long terms to meet your goals.And believe me how happy and boosted you will be in your desired state odf body ,these cheat meals are no big joy.

9.Ask yourself if you are really hungry:

Ahaahaa!!This is one everyone can relate coz sometimes we eat even if we can still resist or not want to.Try preventing these situations.

10.Never say I don't instead I can't:

We say this often that we no longer eat some stuff.But it creates a reverse thought process in mind instead say I can not eat that.

11.Don't gossip or Just take photos:

I promise you the change in your body will guve you much much happiness than by sharing gossips and taking selfies(Even I realised It quite later).
